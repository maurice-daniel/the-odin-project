PISTACHIO CHEESECAKE

Pistachio cheesecake is a creamy dessert with a nutty pistachio flavor, a buttery crust, and a rich pistachio-infused cream cheese filling.

  • 1 ½ cups crushed graham crackers or shortbread cookies
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup sour cream (optional)
  • ½ cup pistachio paste
  • ¼ cup finely chopped pistachios (for garnish)
  • Whipped cream (optional, for garnish)

PROCEDURE.

  1. Prepare the crust: In a bowl, mix the crushed graham crackers or shortbread cookies with melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form an even layer. Refrigerate while you prepare the filling.
  2. Make the cheesecake filling: In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract, sour cream (if using), and pistachio paste. Mix until fully combined.
  3. Bake the cheesecake: Pour the pistachio filling over the crust. Bake in a preheated oven at 325°F (163°C) for 50–60 minutes, or until the center is set and slightly jiggles when shaken. Allow the cheesecake to cool at room temperature before refrigerating for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Garnish: Once the cheesecake has chilled, top it with finely chopped pistachios and optional whipped cream for added flavor and presentation.
  5. Serve: Remove the cheesecake from the springform pan, slice, and enjoy the nutty pistachio goodness.
