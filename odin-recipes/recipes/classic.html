CLASSIC CHEESECAKE

Classic cheesecake is a timeless dessert with a buttery graham cracker crust and a smooth, creamy filling made from rich cream cheese.

  • 1 ½ cups crushed graham crackers (or digestive biscuits)
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 2 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup sour cream (optional)

PROCEDURE.

  1. Prepare the crust: In a bowl, mix the crushed graham crackers and sugar (if using). Add the melted butter and stir until the mixture is well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form an even layer. Refrigerate while you prepare the filling.
  2. Make the cheesecake filling: In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract and sour cream (if using), then mix until fully combined.
  3. Bake the cheesecake: Pour the cheesecake filling over the crust. Bake in a preheated oven at 325°F (163°C) for 50–60 minutes, or until the center is set and slightly jiggles when shaken. Allow the cheesecake to cool at room temperature before transferring to the refrigerator to chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Serve: Once the cheesecake has chilled, remove it from the springform pan, slice, and serve plain or with your favorite toppings like fresh fruit, whipped cream, or chocolate drizzle.
