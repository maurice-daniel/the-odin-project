CHOCOLATE CHEESECAKE

Chocolate cheesecake is a decadent dessert with a chocolate cookie crust, rich chocolate cream cheese filling, and a luscious chocolate ganache topping.

  • 1 ½ cups crushed chocolate cookies (like Oreos, without the filling)
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup sour cream (optional)
  • 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate, melted and slightly cooled
  • ½ cup heavy cream (for ganache topping)
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate (for ganache topping)

PROCEDURE.

  1. Prepare the crust: In a bowl, mix the crushed chocolate cookies and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan to form an even layer. Refrigerate while you prepare the filling.
  2. Make the cheesecake filling: In a large bowl, beat the softened cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract, sour cream (if using), and melted chocolate. Mix until fully combined.
  3. Bake the cheesecake: Pour the chocolate filling over the crust. Bake in a preheated oven at 325°F (163°C) for 50–60 minutes, or until the center is set and slightly jiggles when shaken. Allow the cheesecake to cool at room temperature before refrigerating for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Prepare the chocolate ganache topping: In a small saucepan, heat the heavy cream until just simmering. Remove from heat and pour over the chopped semi-sweet chocolate. Let it sit for a minute, then stir until smooth and glossy. Allow it to cool slightly.
  5. Assemble the cheesecake: Once the cheesecake has chilled, pour the ganache over the top, spreading it evenly. Refrigerate for an additional 30 minutes to set.
  6. Serve: Remove the cheesecake from the springform pan, slice, and serve with optional garnishes like whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or fresh berries.
